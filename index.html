Travelling

Travelling is going to different places instead of staying at the place we are from.There are different modes of transporation to travel. Personally,I enjoy travelling a lot because it allows me to learn about different places, cultures and different kind of lifestyles. Besides, it helps me recuperate from the stress of my private life as well as the boredom of following the monotonous daily routines of my life.
